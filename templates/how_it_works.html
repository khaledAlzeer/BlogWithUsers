Welcome to Khaled Blog!

This blog is a space where I share thoughts about tech, coding, and data analysis. You can explore posts, follow tutorials, and learn new things.

Visitor (Not logged in)

  • Read all posts freely.
  • Cannot comment or interact with posts.
  • To comment or create posts, you need to register or log in.

Registered User

  • Can comment on posts.
  • Can view their own profile (optional).
  • Cannot delete or edit other posts.

Admin (Me)

  • Can create, edit, and delete any post.
  • Can manage contact messages.
  • Has full access to site content management.
